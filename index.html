About Me

Hello All, I am Shahzad Khan, a Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Temple University. Some of my current interests include tinkering, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. I am currently the President of Temple University's IEEE Student Branch, where we host both professional and workshop events for students and my main driving factor is to create a community for students to learn and grow in the field of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

Throughout my time at Temple I have worked on a variety of projects, but now I have started to sway to the combination of hardware and software and from that I have gained an interest in DSP, GPUs and that has been reflected in my current classes.
